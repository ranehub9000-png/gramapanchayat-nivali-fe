Welcome to Nivali Gram Panchayat

Nivali Gram Panchayat serves as the administrative and governance hub for the village of Nivali and surrounding areas. Our office is dedicated to providing essential services, implementing government schemes, and fostering community development.

Nestled in the picturesque landscape of Chiplun Taluka, Ratnagiri District, Nivali is part of the beautiful Konkan region of Maharashtra. Surrounded by rolling hills, lush greenery, and scenic vistas, our village represents the rich cultural heritage and natural beauty of rural Maharashtra.

The region is known for its fertile lands, agricultural prosperity, and close-knit community. Our Gram Panchayat is committed to empowering our rural community through transparent governance, citizen-centric services, and sustainable development initiatives that preserve our heritage while embracing progress.

About Our Location

Nivali Gram Panchayat is situated in the heart of Ratnagiri District, within the administrative jurisdiction of Chiplun Taluka. Our village is part of the scenic Konkan region, characterized by its verdant landscapes, agricultural abundance, and traditional rural lifestyle.

The area is well-connected by road networks, making it easily accessible from nearby towns and cities.
